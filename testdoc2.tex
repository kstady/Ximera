\begin{question}
\textrm{A normal seated adult breathes in and exhales about 0.8 L of air every 4 seconds.  An experiment produced the graph below.  The volume $V$ of air
in the lungs $t$ seconds after exhaling can be approximated by a cosine function of the form $V= A \cos Bt + D, $  $0 \le t \le 8.$   Find the equation. }
%\textbf{Explain your reasoning and show your work.}  %Answer:  -0.35*cos(deg(pi*x/2)) + 0.45



\vspace{0.2in}
\begin{image}

\end{question}
